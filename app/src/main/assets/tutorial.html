Goal

To score points, place six pieces into pies to create complete circles. In order to successfully place a piece, there must be an open section of the pie that matches the shape of the piece. Do this as quickly as you can, the clock is ticking!

Scoring

When you complete a pie, you get 60 points (10 points per piece). If all of the pieces are the same color, the score increases to 120 points (20 points per piece).

Power Ups

When you complete a pie with a single color that has a power up above it, that power up immediately becomes available. The three power ups are:

  • 2x: all points you score while this power up is active will be multiplied by 2.
  • The Bomb: tapping this power up will clear the game board of all pieces. You will receive no points for the pieces cleared.
  • Slow Down: slows down the speed of the piece timer by 50%.
All power ups are valid for 15 seconds.

Lives

Every time you complete a pie with pieces of a single color, you gain one life. Every time you fail to place a piece, or skip a piece by tapping the center pie, you lose one life.

Tips

Some aspects of game play can be tweaked in the Settings screen.
If you want to end a game in a hurry, press and hold on the Pause button.
